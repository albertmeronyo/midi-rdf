MIDI to RDF conversion; link it to the cloud!

Convert your MIDI file to an RDF Turtle file, using this service version of the midi2rdf suite of converters. Simply drag and drop your MIDI file to the box below!

Tick the checkbox if you also want your RDFied MIDI to be integrated in a growing collection of more than 6 billion triples of symbolic music information. You may want to do this because:

  • Your MIDIs will become richer, pointing to (and being pointed from) shared musical resources in the MIDI-LD cloud
  • Your MIDIs will get free metadata (e.g. links to the Wikipedia page of the song they contain)
  • Your MIDIs will contribute to a growing effort of understanding and using music in artificial intelligence research
  • Your MIDIs will get provenance links with proper attribution
  • We converted your MIDIs to RDF for free; we take your consent to link them as a donation! :-)
